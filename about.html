About Arya P.G. College

A legacy of academic excellence since 1965

Our History & Mission

Founded in 1965, Arya P.G. College has been a beacon of higher education in Panipat, Haryana. Established with the vision of providing quality education to the youth of the region, our college has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected institutions in the state.

Our mission is to empower students through holistic education that combines academic rigor with character building. We strive to create an environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and social responsibility.

Our Journey

Milestones in our history of educational excellence

1965 - Foundation

Arya P.G. College was established with just two departments and a vision to provide quality higher education in Panipat.

1978 - Expansion

Added Science and Commerce streams, becoming a full-fledged degree college offering undergraduate programs.

1992 - Postgraduate Programs

Started offering postgraduate courses in Arts and Science disciplines, elevating our academic profile.

2005 - New Campus

Moved to our current 15-acre campus with modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities.

2018 - NAAC Accreditation

Awarded 'A' grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council, recognizing our academic excellence.
