Department of Science

The Science Department at Arya P.G. College is committed to fostering scientific inquiry and innovation. Our faculty members are dedicated researchers and educators who provide students with a strong foundation in scientific principles.

We offer state-of-the-art laboratories, research opportunities, and industry collaborations to ensure our students receive hands-on experience with modern scientific techniques and technologies.

Department of Commerce

The Commerce Department at Arya P.G. College prepares students for successful careers in business, finance, and accounting. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

We emphasize developing analytical skills, business acumen, and ethical decision-making through case studies, projects, and industry interactions. Our graduates are well-prepared for the dynamic world of commerce and business.
